EMPOWER, UNITE & TRANSORM

The Iftiin Foundation for Social Innovation & Entrepreneurship (IFFSIE), a Somalia-founded non-profit, empowers young people in violence-affected communities to become leaders in building peaceful, prosperous societies.

We operate across three mission areas to achieve greater impact: Economic Freedom, Mental Health, and Systems Change.

Market-driven skills training
